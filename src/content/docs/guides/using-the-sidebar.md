title: Using the Sidebar
description: Learn how to navigate and use the dassi sidebar interface.
---

The dassi sidebar is your main interface for interacting with the AI agent. Here's how to get the most out of it.

## Opening the sidebar

There are two ways to open the sidebar:

- **Click the dassi icon** in your Chrome toolbar
- **Use the keyboard shortcut** (configurable in extension settings)

## The chat interface

The sidebar shows a chat interface where you can:

- **Type messages** to give dassi instructions
- **See responses** as dassi reads your page and takes actions
- **Review actions** before dassi executes them

## Conversation history

dassi keeps your conversation history within a session. You can:

- **Scroll up** to see previous messages
- **Start a new conversation** by clicking the **New Chat** button
- **Reference earlier context** — dassi remembers what you've discussed

## Settings

Access settings by clicking the **gear icon** in the sidebar header:

- **AI Provider** — Switch between connected providers
- **Model** — Choose which model to use
- **Preferences** — Customize dassi's behavior

## Tips

- **Keep the sidebar open** while working — dassi can monitor and assist as you browse
- **Be conversational** — you can follow up and refine requests naturally
- **Use it on any page** — dassi works on any website you visit
