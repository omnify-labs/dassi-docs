dassi is a Chrome extension. Installation takes less than a minute.

## Requirements

- **Google Chrome** version 138 or later
- A **Google account** for signing in

No API key is required to get started — dassi includes managed credits out of the box.

## Install from Chrome Web Store

1. Visit the [dassi Chrome Web Store page](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/dassi-ai-browser-agent/bjcngahpcjeililljmfegmlanlpgibdi)
2. Click **Add to Chrome**
3. Confirm the installation when prompted

## First-time setup

After installation, dassi opens a short onboarding walkthrough:

1. **Welcome** — dassi introduces itself
2. **Find the extension** — locate the extensions icon in your Chrome toolbar
3. **Pin the extension** — pin dassi for quick access
4. **Get started** — open dassi and start your first conversation

## Pin the extension

For quick access, pin dassi to your toolbar:

1. Click the **puzzle piece icon** (Extensions) in the Chrome toolbar
2. Find **dassi** in the list
3. Click the **pin icon** next to dassi

## Sign in

The first time you open the sidebar, you'll see a **Sign in with Google** button. Sign in to activate your account and receive managed credits.

## Next steps

Once signed in, you're ready to go. Try the [Quick Start](/getting-started/quick-start/) guide to complete your first task.
